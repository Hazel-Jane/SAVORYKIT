WEATHER NUTRITION TIPS

‚òÄÔ∏è SUNNY: Beat the Heat!

  • Hydration is Key: Drink plenty of fluids to replenish what is lost through sweat.
  • Light & Water-Rich Dishes: Choose foods that are easy to digest and naturally cooling.
  • Hydrating Recipes:
    • Fruit Shake (Watermelon, Mango), Mango Tapioca, Buko Pandan: Excellent for high water content and natural sugars for energy.
    • Halo-Halo, Mais con yelo: Cold desserts that provide immediate cooling and hydration.
    • Fruit Salad, Atchara, Kinilaw: Light, vinegar-based or raw dishes that are refreshing and low-fat.
    • Sinigang na Isda: The sour broth is light and tart, which is great for hot days.
    • Also great: Fresh lumpia, salads, ensaladang talong.

üåßÔ∏è RAINY: Warmth & Immunity

  • Warm & Comforting: Warm liquids and hearty meals help raise body temperature.
  • Immunity Boosters: Prioritize dishes rich in ginger, garlic, and Vitamin C to ward off colds.
  • Warming Recipes:
    • Soups/Broths: Lomi, Sopas, Batchoy, Sinabawang Manok, Lugaw (Arroz Caldo style), Sinigang na Baboy: These are hot, soothing, and often contain ginger or garlic.
    • Heavy Comfort Foods:Champorado (best warm), Hot Cocoa, Kape (Coffee), Tea: Provide warmth and emotional comfort.
    • Savory & Hearty: Beef Stew, Kaldereta, Ramen (Filipino Style), Lasagna: Rich, warm dishes to fuel the body on cold days.
    • Flavorful & Rich: Bicol Express, Dinuguan, Adobong Manok, Sisig: These rich flavors are comforting in cool weather.
